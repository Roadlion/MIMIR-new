Cross-Sectional Operators 1 arg

Operate across all tickers on each date independently.

FunctionReturnsDescription
rank(x) DataFrame Cross-sectional percentile rank (0.0–1.0). Non-zero elements only; zeros remain 0.
scale(x) DataFrame Divides each row by its absolute sum → weights sum to 1.0 across tickers.
neutralize(x) DataFrame Subtracts the cross-sectional mean → dollar-neutral portfolio weights.
zscore(x) DataFrame Cross-sectional z-score: (x − μ) / σ. Standardizes across tickers per day.

Time-Series Operators 2 args

Rolling window functions: func(dataframe, window_days). d must be a positive integer (except ts_decay_exp: float halflife).

FunctionAliasDescription
ts_delay(x, d)delayShift x backward by d days (NaN pad at start).
ts_delta(x, d)—Difference: x − ts_delay(x, d).
ts_mean(x, d)—Simple moving average over d days.
ts_std(x, d)ts_std_devRolling standard deviation over d days.
ts_sum(x, d)—Rolling sum over d days.
ts_max(x, d)—Rolling maximum over d days.
ts_min(x, d)—Rolling minimum over d days.
ts_rank(x, d)—Rolling percentile rank of latest value within window.
ts_zscore(x, d)—Rolling z-score: (x − rolling_mean) / rolling_std.
ts_decay_linear(x, d)—Linearly-weighted decay (recent → higher weight).
ts_decay_exp(x, d)—Exponential weighted moving average. d = halflife (float).
ts_argmax(x, d)—Days since maximum value in window (0 = today).
ts_argmin(x, d)—Days since minimum value in window (0 = today).
ts_skewness(x, d)—Rolling skewness of distribution in window.
ts_kurtosis(x, d)—Rolling excess kurtosis in window.
ts_product(x, d)—Rolling product (∏) over d days.
ts_min_max_scale(x, d)—Scale to [0, 1]: (x − min) / (max − min) over window.
ts_av_diff(x, d)—Deviation from rolling mean: x − ts_mean(x, d).

Multi-Arg & Special Operators 2–3 args

FunctionArgsDescription
returns(d) 1 Close % change over d days. Shorthand for close.pct_change(d). Also callable as returns(x, d) on any DataFrame.
if_else(cond, x, y) 3 Ternary: where cond is True use x, else y. Element-wise.
clamp(x, lo, hi) 3 Clip x to [lo, hi]. Equivalent to max(lo, min(x, hi)).
trade_when(t, a, e) 3 Stateful entry/exit. Enter position a when trigger t is True. Hold until exit e is True (→ NaN).
ts_corr(y, x, d) 3 Rolling Pearson correlation of y and x over d days. Alias: correlation.
ts_covariance(y, x, d) 3 Rolling covariance of y and x over d days.
ts_regression_beta(y, x, d) 3 Rolling β: Cov(y, x) / Var(x) over d days.
ts_regression_alpha(y, x, d) 3 Rolling α: mean(y) − β · mean(x) over d days.
ts_regression_residual(y, x, d) 3 Rolling residual: y − (β·x + α) over d days.
