Formula Cheat Sheet

sentiment

Raw news sentiment scores.

social_chatter

Raw social chatter sentiment scores.

returns(d)

Asset percentage returns over d days.

neutralize(x)

De-means weights to make the portfolio dollar-neutral.

scale(x)

Scales absolute sum of weights to 1.0 (leverage factor).

rank(x)

Cross-sectional percentile rank (0.0 to 1.0) of values.

zscore(x)

Cross-sectional z-score of x: (x - mean) / std.

ts_delay(x, d)

Shifts the data stream back by d days (Alias: delay).

ts_delta(x, d)

Time-series difference: x - ts_delay(x, d).

ts_mean(x, d)

Time-series rolling average of x over d days.

ts_std_dev(x, d)

Time-series rolling standard deviation (Alias: ts_std).

ts_decay_linear(x, d)

Linear weighted decay rolling sum of weights over d days.

ts_zscore(x, d)

Rolling time-series z-score over d days.

ts_corr(x, y, d)

Rolling correlation of x and y over d days (Alias: correlation).

ts_covariance(x, y, d)

Rolling covariance of x and y over d days.

if_else(cond, x, y)

Ternary selection: returns x where cond is true, else y.

abs(x)

Absolute value of x.

log(x)

Natural logarithm of x.

exp(x)

Exponential (e^x) of x.

sqrt(x)

Square root of x.

sign(x)

Returns sign indicator: 1.0, -1.0, or 0.0.
